How it Started

It all began when someone confided in two of his friends and told them he was suffering from severe anxiety and depression. Naturally, his friends were concerned, and they asked carefully if he was willing to take the step to talk to someone professional. With a look of defeat, he told them he had spent weeks looking for a therapist within his budget with no avail. This look of defeat was what made his two friends work hand in hand to bring this concept to life.
